Religion

As of 2015:

  • 67.8% are irreligious
  • 11.7% are roman catholic
  • 8.6% are protestant
  • 4.2% are of another christian denomination
  • 5.8% are islam
  • 2% are hindu or buddhist

The Netherlands declared independence from Spain in 1581, and became a large commercial power in the 17th century. They then regained independence from the french in 1813. They now export a large amount of agricultural products.
